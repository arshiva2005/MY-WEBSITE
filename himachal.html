Explore Himachal Pradesh

Popular Places

Manali

A popular hill station known for its stunning landscapes, adventure sports, and tranquility.

Shimla

The capital city known for its colonial architecture and beautiful hill views.

Dharamshala

A serene town known for its Tibetan culture and the residence of the Dalai Lama.

Kullu

Famous for its beautiful valleys, rivers, and adventure sports like paragliding and rafting.

Manikaran

A pilgrimage town known for its hot springs and beautiful temples.
