Explore Amritsar

Popular Places

Golden Temple

The holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, famous for its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere.

Jallianwala Bagh

A public garden memorializing the victims of the 1919 massacre, showcasing rich history.

Wagah Border

Famous for the daily flag-lowering ceremony performed by the Indian and Pakistani military.

Partition Museum

A museum dedicated to the history and impact of the Partition of India in 1947.

Sikh Museum

A museum highlighting the rich history of Sikhism through various artifacts and exhibits.
