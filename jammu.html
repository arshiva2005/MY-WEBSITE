Explore Jammu

Popular Places

Vaishno Devi

A sacred pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi, located in the Trikuta Mountains.

Patnitop

A beautiful hill station known for its lush meadows and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Bahu Fort

A historical fort that offers panoramic views of the city and the Tawi River, with a beautiful garden.

Bhaderwah

Known as the 'Mini Kashmir', Bhaderwah is famous for its picturesque landscapes and rich culture.

Old City

Experience the vibrant culture and history of Jammu in its old city with ancient temples and bustling bazaars.
